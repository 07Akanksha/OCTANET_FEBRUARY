About Us

Serving Since 1950

Our Story

The history of coffee dates back to centuries of old oral tradition in modern day Ethiopia. However, neither where coffee was first cultivated nor direct evidence of its consumption prior to the 15th century have been found. Sufi monasteries in Yemen employed coffee as an aid to concentration during prayers. Coffee later spread to the Levant and Persia in the early 16th century; it caused some controversy on whether it was halal in Ottoman and Mamluk society.

Our Vision

Coffeehouses were an important part of the counterculture movement in the 1950's. Today coffeehouses are a major part of mainstream American culture.We have been producing since 1950 in the heart of Tuscany, enhancing the characteristics of the beans with care and passion, heirs of an artisan tradition that creates value and knows how to recognize true beauty. Even in a cup of coffee.
